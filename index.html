Sripathi Panditaradhyula Balasubrahmanyam (4 June 1946 – 25 September 2020),
also referred to as S. P. B. or Balu, was an Indian musician, playback singer, television anchor, music director, actor, dubbing artist, and film producer who worked predominantly in Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Hindi, and Malayalam films. for more see above link that says know more about S.P.B.
On 25 September 2020, he died in Chennai from post COVID-19 complications.
